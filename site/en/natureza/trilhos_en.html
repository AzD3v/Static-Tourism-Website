Tracks in Sete Cidades

Vista do Rei track

Obviously the route begins in the Vista do Rei viewpoint, ending in the parish and it has a total duration of about 2 hours. It starts in the west of the peak of the Sete Cidades boiler. In this part of the track one has a view of the right side of the Caldeira Seca and the lagoons and on the left side the west coast of the island.

Following the dirty track, the traveler arrives at an tarmac road, which connects the partish of Mosteiros to the parish of Sete Cidades. Following up there is an curve to the right and an downhill which ends at a new dirty road. Thereafter the rider will find an bifurcation where the track intersects with the PR4 SMI route(as you can see on the map bellow). Arriving at that location, there is an curve to right and one walks down until arrival at the Sete Cidades parish.

Summary:


Category: Linear

Dificulty: Easy

Extension: 7.7 km

Duration: About 2h



Mata do Canário track

Starting around the surrouinds of the Mata do Canário, the route begins with a downhill of dirt, which continues until an tarmac road. Turning left there is an straight road of 300 meters until the Muro das Nove Janelas. Following up, the traveler follows an left path, between the cryptomies. After an while, the traveler climbs at a road that ends at the first Viewpoint with a view to the lagoons, at Pico da Cruz. From here, there is a path that bypass the lagoon of bluish color. 8 km later, one will find an information that indicates an detour of 1,2 km to the left. Thereafter, the traveler begins an downhill that will end up at the Sete Cidades parish. On this area of the route some caution is advised, due to the fact of the floor being slippery and inclined. Upon arrival at the parish, the traveler turns right at the bridge and follows the path for 500m until the church, where the track ends.

Summary:


Category: Linear

Dificulty: Easy

Extension: 11.8 km

Duration: About 3h



Track of Serra Devassa

The track begins with an easy climb, skirting a small swamp and after that there is a harder ascend until arrival at an geodetic zone (geographic divisions of the land), which points out the higher summit of the track. Along this rise one can observe, to the left, the Muro das Nove Janelas and the lagoon of Pau Pique, localizated at the center of a small volcanic cone.



This track takes place in a area of altitude comprehended between 750m and 900m, reason why it's advisable to do it in days of good visibility.

Summary


Category: Circular

Dificulty: Medium

Extension: 5km

Duration: About 2h
