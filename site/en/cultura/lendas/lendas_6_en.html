Legends of Sete Cidades

Legend of the princess and the shepherd of the Sete Cidades Kingdom

The kings of the supposed beautiful land of Sete Cidades had a rebellious daughter who didn’t like to be between the walls of the castle and went every day to the outside. She loved the the flowers, the birds, as much as the sea on the horizon. She walked through the villages, the hills and the valleys.

During one of his trips she met a shepherd, from a simple family. During their meetings, they talked about the things of life, discovering that they had many things in common. Soon, they fell in love for each other. However, the princess already had a wedding arranged by her parents with a prince of a neighbor kingdom. When her father knew of these meetings with the shepherd, he immediately prohibited them, giving her daughter a final date him the pastor.

According to the legend, in this last meeting they cried so much that bellow their gradually started to grow two ponds. One of the ponds, the one with bluish-colored waters, was born of the tears shed by the blue eyes of the princess. The other, green, was born from the tears shed by the green eyes of the shepherd.

This legend Sete Cidades has as its primary objective explain the difference between the two colors of the lakes present at the parish.

The fascinium, magic and allurement in perfect symbiosis
