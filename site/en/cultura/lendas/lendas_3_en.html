Legends of Sete Cidades

Legend of the Bishop Genádio

Genádio was the name of a boy who had a youth adventures, a spoiled child with a powerful father. One day he discovered that he had special powers, particularly the power of necromancer (the ability to communicate with the spiritual world and retrieve information about the past, the future and the postmortem).

One day, Genásio got tired of the life he had and decided to become a priest and escape the social world, consecrating his life and whole existence to God. Gathering their capabilities of necromancer to his new religious activity, he became famous, obtaining the status of Archbishop. Thanks to his skills, he earned the title of Bishop later.

One night a newborn child was put at the door of Genádio’s church. Collected by him, the baby girl was treated with all the affection the Bishop could give. It was at this time that the muslims stormed the Iberian Peninsula. Sensing the danger, Genádio decided to gather all his disciples and leave. He built a fleet of large ships and left for the occidental Great Sea, accompanied by the girl he had adopted and a large number of citizens. After many days sailling in the ocean, he arrived at an unknown island. At this Island Genádio and his followers docked their ships and deciding to declare it their territory, founding a great city and, for each of the seven archbishops who were part of the crew, they called it Sete Cidades.

After many years, the girl grew up and dreamed every day of returning to the great and distant continent which she fled a long time before. In order to protect her from any harm, Genádio used his powers to hide the Island from those who approached it.

One morning there was a caravel on the horizon, bringing the cross of the order of Christ. Furious, Genádio couldn’t understand the reason why his powers were failing. When the ship was already close to the docking bay, the Bishop appealed to the extremes of his power. Suddenly and without warning, the beautiful island became a huge and angry vulcan that exploded, destroying everything around it.

After that, and in memory of the few survivors, it was told the legend of island of Sete Cidades that had been founded by Genádio, and by his followers.
