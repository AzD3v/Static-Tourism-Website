A paradise in the middle of the ocean

Sete Cidades it's a portuguese hall from Ponta Delgada, Azores, with 19,22 km² and aproximatly 800 habitats (acording to records in 2011). His population density is about 41,3 habitats per km²

Located on a 37.87 latitude north and 205.78 longitude west, with aproximatly 260 meters of altitude on the bottom of the vulcanos crater, in the estern margin of the tow lagoons, that has the same name of the "city" ans its concidered to be of the most beautiful spots of Portugal and the entire world. This is the main atraction for tourism.

It's located precisely on flat margin of the blue lagoon, the city has many tipical houses of the region giving a small sense of nostalgia to whoever passes by. It also has some beautiful arquitecture like Igreja de São Nicolau, having a neo-Gothic style, buit around 1852, not leaving out the casa dos herdeiros de Caetano de Andrade, and of corse, the tunel ..., dated 1937.
